About Wision Visuals

At Wision Visuals, we don't just capture moments - we craft cinematic stories.

Founded by Wadood Alshekh, a filmmaker trained at Budapest Metropolitan Film School, we bring together classical aesthetics with modern editing to deliver visually rich narratives across commercials, weddings, and social media.

With a strong focus on commercials, event coverage, social media content, and professional editing, Wadood blends classical cinematic style with modern aesthetics - using limited gear but boundless imagination. Every project is approached with dedication and storytelling at its core.

Wadood Alshekh

Syrian filmmaker based in Germany with a passion for visual storytelling. His journey began in Hungary, where he studied film and built a strong visual language rooted in emotion, rhythm, and cinematic precision.
